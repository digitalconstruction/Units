Description

Digital Construction Units vocabulary defines units and quantity kinds that are specific to construction and renovation projects, for instance, concerning energy simulation results.

Overview

Digital Construction Units vocabulary defines quantity kind and unit of measure individuals that are relevant in construction and renovation domain but not available in the Units and Quantitykind vocabularies of QUDT . Examples are various energy efficiency and cost related units.

Note that as this module is a vocabulary, it only specifies named individuals (instances of classes), and does not define any new classes or properties.
